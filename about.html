About NovomindAI

Pioneering the future of human-AI interaction

Our Mission

At NovomindAI, we believe that artificial intelligence should enhance human capabilities, not replace them. Our mission is to create AI systems that understand context, emotion, and nuance - making every interaction feel genuinely human.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of AI researchers and engineers, NovomindAI started as a research project at Stanford University. Today, we serve thousands of businesses worldwide, helping them transform customer experiences through intelligent conversation.
